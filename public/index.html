About Me

I come from an electrical engineering background of 3 years, specializing in embedded programming and signal integrity. I am proficient in back-end design, with a talent for pattern recognition and modification - that is, I can identify something similar to a problem I am trying to solve, see how that process works, modify it to my situation, and implement that modified variant to resolve an issue. I am currently working towards pivoting from my current focus in hardware into a more software-oriented role because the experience I've had with coding and software design has been more interesting and fulfilling than engaging solely with hardware, and I believe that my engineering background and outlook would be a benefit to any team.
